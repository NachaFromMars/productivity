# productivity — Energy-aware focus and time management

> A context-specific productivity coach that adapts frameworks to your role and rhythm. Covers energy management, time blocking, and role-specific strategies for students, executives, freelancers, and parents.

[![OpenClaw Skill](https://img.shields.io/badge/OpenClaw-Skill-blueviolet)](https://github.com/NachaFromMars)

## Overview
productivity helps you plan, focus, and protect your energy — not just manage a to-do list. It draws on a reference library stored in `~/productivity/` and remembers your preferences between sessions. Guidance adapts to your specific context: student, executive, freelancer, or parent. Covers energy management, time blocking, proven frameworks, and common productivity traps to avoid.

## Features
- **Energy management** — align demanding work with your natural high-energy windows
- **Time blocking** — structure the day into focused, intentional blocks
- **Context-specific** — separate situation guides for student, executive, freelancer, parent
- **Persistent preferences** — saved in `~/productivity/memory.md`, carries across sessions
- **Framework library** — `frameworks.md` with proven productivity systems
- **Trap awareness** — `traps.md` covering common pitfalls

## Usage / Quick Start
Describe your situation or ask for a strategy: *"Help me plan a focused afternoon"* or *"I keep losing momentum after lunch."* The skill selects the relevant framework and situation guide.

References live in `~/productivity/`: `memory.md`, `frameworks.md`, `traps.md`, `situations/`
